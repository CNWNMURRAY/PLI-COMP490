Register to Host A Clinic

Chicago Advocate Legal (CAL) is looking to partner with organizations who provide support/services to low and middle income communities and individuals who are most likely to be self represented in court. CAL will offer an educational workshop for your organization and community members. Attorneys from CAL and volunteers/interns will facilitate the workshop in a location of the host sites choosing. Attorneys will usually be there to answer more specific questions related to legal issues while volunteers/interns will provide indepth legal education to your staff and community members on civil court process and procedures for housing and family law issues.

How It Works

Please fill out the form below if you would like to host a clinic. Remember to be a host site your organization must:
1. be able to provide free space;
2. must have a minimum of 10 participants;
3. it is recommended that the organization advertise the event for a minimum of one month. Contact Us @ Chicago Advocate Legal, NFP or 312-561-5510
