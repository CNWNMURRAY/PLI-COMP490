The Preventative Law Initiative



The Preventative Law Initiative is a project focusing on reducing the finacial and emtional cost of civil ligitgation on self represented litigants by prodividng legal information education. Chicago Advocate Legal, NFP (CAL),provides legal education workshops to the community on various housing and family law topics.


The Preventative E-Learning Module is designed to improve non-lawyers understanding of the civil legal process.


Chicago Advocate Legal, NFP is a non-profit law firm Chicago Advocate Legal, NFP is dedicated to providing affordable, convenient, and comprehensive legal services. Chicago Advocate Legal, NFP is putting access to justice back in your hands.
